About SoftBooks

A free digital library built with one goal — make reading accessible to everyone, everywhere.

👨‍💻

Built by Kiran Sankar

Founder of KS Tech · Built with ❤️ + 🤖 AI assistance

🚀 The Mission

SoftBooks is part of the KS Tech ecosystem — a growing collection of free, clean, no-nonsense tools built for everyone. Our goal is simple: give every student and reader free access to quality books, textbooks, and knowledge — with zero ads, zero sign-ups, and zero nonsense.

📚 What is SoftBooks?

SoftBooks is a free book discovery and reading platform. We curate links to publicly available PDFs from trusted open-access sources like Project Gutenberg and Internet Archive. From NCERT textbooks to classic novels — it's all here, free forever.

🤖 AI-Assisted Build

SoftBooks was designed and developed with the help of AI tools. The "Lemme Suggest" feature uses a smart preference-matching algorithm to recommend books based on your age, mood, genre preferences, reading level, and more. The future of building is human creativity + AI speed.

🔮 What's Coming

More subjects (Maths, CS, Economics), more classes (8, 9, 10), competitive exam resources (NEET, JEE, UPSC), Hindi and regional language books, non-academic categories (finance, self-help, literature), and a full mobile app — all in the roadmap.

⚖️ Legal & Copyright

SoftBooks does not host, store, or redistribute any copyrighted files. All book links point to publicly available, legally free PDFs on third-party open-access platforms. All books are either in the public domain or freely distributed by their publishers. If you believe a link violates copyright, please contact us.
